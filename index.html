More Info About Kogyo

What stage is Kogyo in?

We currently have a prototype of the Kogyo app developed, featuring the core functionality of matching helpers with tasks. However, it still lacks some important additional features. To advance the business, we are exploring joining an accelerator or raising pre-seed funding to complete the app's development and gain initial traction.

What is Kogyo's target market?

Kogyo will initially target Southeast Asia (SEA), launching first in Thailand before expanding throughout the region. Although our initial focus is on SEA, we plan to take Kogyo worldwide, as we believe it addresses a universal problem that people everywhere face.

But don't Taskrabbit, Thumbtack, and Airtasker do the same thing?

While apps like TaskRabbit, Thumbtack, and Airtasker operate in the same industry, they lack critical features that prevent them from truly revolutionizing the market. Kogyo's unique pay-by-job system fills this gap, offering a key advantage that these platforms are missing. Additionally, Kogyo will launch in Southeast Asia, a region where these apps currently do not exist, providing an untapped market opportunity.
